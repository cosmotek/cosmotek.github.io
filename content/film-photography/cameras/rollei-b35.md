The Rollei B35 is one of the smallest full-frame 35mm cameras ever made, and it still feels incredibly well built for its size. No rangefinder, just zone focusing and a built-in light meter - it forces you to slow down and guess distances, which is part of the fun.

## Why I Love It

- Genuinely pocketable, solid German engineering
- Sharp little Triotar lens
- Great for quick, unobtrusive street shots
- Looks as good as it shoots

## Tips for Using It

1. Practice zone focusing by pacing off distances until it becomes second nature
2. Works best in good light given the fixed f/3.5 lens
3. Keep the selenium meter out of direct sun when metering for accurate readings
4. A wrist strap helps since there's no grip to speak of
