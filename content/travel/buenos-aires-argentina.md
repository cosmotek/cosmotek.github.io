Buenos Aires feels like a South American metropolis with a European soul: wide boulevards, café-lined streets, and an energetic nightlife. I wandered San Telmo's antique markets, watched tango in intimate venues, and enjoyed parrilla dinners that stretch late into the night.

Take time to explore Palermo's parks and restaurants, and book popular shows or restaurants ahead of weekends. The city's cultural calendar makes any visit feel lively and layered.
