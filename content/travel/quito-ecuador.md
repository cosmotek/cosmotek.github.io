Quito sits high in the Andean valley with a beautifully preserved historic center full of churches and plazas. I enjoyed wandering the colonial streets, visiting hilltop viewpoints, and using the city as a base for nearby cloud forest and mountain excursions. Be mindful of altitude and take it easy on your first day.

Local markets and street food offer approachable flavors, and day trips can include the Middle of the World monument or nearby nature reserves.
