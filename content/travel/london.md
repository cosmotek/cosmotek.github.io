London resists simple summaries — it's a mosaic of neighborhoods, museums, and culinary diversity. I spent mornings in museum districts and afternoons exploring markets like Borough and Portobello Road. Each borough has its own personality, from the historic core around Westminster to edgy Shoreditch.

Plan for public transport and wear comfortable shoes; the Underground and buses make it easy to move between distant corners of the city. Book theater tickets and popular attractions in advance.
