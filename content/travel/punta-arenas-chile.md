Punta Arenas sits near the tip of South America and serves as a practical base for exploring nearby wildlife and historic sites. I visited nearby penguin colonies and learned about the city's maritime history at local museums. The Patagonian winds are a defining feature; plan clothing layers and give yourself extra travel time for ferries and weather-dependent trips.

Punta Arenas is also a logistical hub for longer expeditions — book transportation and excursions through reputable operators.
