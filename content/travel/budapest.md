Budapest offers a striking mix of architecture and relaxed social life. The thermal baths are a must — Széchenyi and Gellért provide restorative warmth in dramatic settings. Walk along the Danube at sunset and take in the illuminated Parliament building, or dive into the bohemian energy of the ruin bars in the Jewish Quarter.

Local food like lángos and hearty goulash are perfect after a day of sightseeing. Consider a river cruise for a different perspective on the city's bridges and skyline.
