Malmö sits a short train ride from Copenhagen and rewards visitors with relaxed parks, waterfront promenades, and inventive local restaurants. The city is pedestrian-friendly and has a lively cultural scene — visit the Turning Torso skyline, stroll through Gamla Väster, and sample falafel from the bustling Möllevången market.

Malmö feels like a smaller, softer complement to Copenhagen. It's an excellent place to explore by foot and appreciate modern Scandinavian urban planning without the tourist crowds.
