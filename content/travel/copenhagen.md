Copenhagen is compact and eminently bikeable, with clean canals, colorful houses in Nyhavn, and world-class dining. I spent mornings cycling between neighborhoods, stopping for smørrebrød and coffee, and evenings lingering in cozy cafés practicing deliberate relaxation — hygge in action.

Don't miss Tivoli Gardens at dusk, a stroll through Christianshavn, and the design shops along Strøget. For food, book ahead for the popular restaurants, or enjoy creative Nordic cuisine at one of the city's many smaller eateries.
