Puerto Natales is the jumping-off point for Torres del Paine, offering access to jaw-dropping mountain scenery and glaciers. I used the town as a base for multi-day treks and day tours into the park; prepare for variable weather and rugged trails. Even the town's waterfront offers beautiful views of the fjord and peaks beyond.

Gear up for cold, wind, and long hikes, and book park permits and transport in advance during high season.
