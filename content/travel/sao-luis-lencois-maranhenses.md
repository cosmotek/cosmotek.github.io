São Luís offers a colonial core of colorful tiled buildings and a strong cultural heritage, while Lençóis Maranhenses — a short trip away — feels like another planet with white sand dunes dotted by translucent freshwater lagoons each rainy season. I combined city exploration with a guided tour into the park to walk the dunes and swim in crystalline pools.

Timing matters: the lagoons are fullest in the months after the rains (roughly June through September), so plan your visit around that window for the best contrast between sand and water.
