Prague's cobblestone streets and fairy-tale architecture make it a delight to wander. From the Charles Bridge at sunrise to the hilltop Prague Castle, the city is full of viewpoints and historic corners. I enjoyed getting lost in the Old Town, sipping coffee in hidden courtyards, and sampling hearty Czech comfort food paired with local beer.

Tip: cross the Charles Bridge early to avoid the crowds, and take time to explore the quieter neighborhoods across the river for a more local feel.
