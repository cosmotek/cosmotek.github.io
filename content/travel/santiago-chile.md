Santiago is a sprawling city with a backdrop of the Andes and diverse neighborhoods to explore. I loved spending time in Bellavista for arts and nightlife, and walking through the leafy Parque Bicentenario. The city's museums and markets provide cultural context for Chile's history and contemporary life.

Vineyards are a short drive away for wine tasting, and day trips to the coast or mountains are easy options for varied scenery.
