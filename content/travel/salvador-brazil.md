Salvador pulses with music, color, and history. The Pelourinho district's colonial architecture and lively street performances create an immersive cultural experience. I sampled local Bahian dishes like acarajé and watched percussion groups practicing in plazas.

Plan for warm coastal weather and explore nearby beaches or cultural tours that highlight Salvador's Afro-Brazilian traditions.
