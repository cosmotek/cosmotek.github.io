title: "Lima, Peru"
date: 2026-07-07
description: "Peru's culinary capital and a city of contrasts."
tags: ["travel", "Peru", "Lima"]
---

Lima is an exciting mix of colonial neighborhoods, modern districts, and a culinary scene that draws food lovers from around the world. I spent evenings sampling ceviche and Nikkei cuisine, and afternoons walking the Miraflores boardwalk with views over the Pacific.

Be sure to visit the historic center for its plazas and cathedrals, and explore Barranco for street art and a bohemian vibe. If you're headed to the Andes, plan time to acclimatize and enjoy Lima's exceptional restaurants beforehand.
