Rio mixes dramatic geography with infectious energy: sugarloaf peaks, Christ the Redeemer, and famous beaches like Ipanema and Copacabana. I enjoyed morning walks along the shore, afternoon hikes with sweeping views, and lively evenings exploring music and food scenes.

Safety-wise, stay aware of your surroundings and ask locals about neighborhoods to avoid after dark. With sensible planning, Rio's beauty and culture make it unforgettable.
