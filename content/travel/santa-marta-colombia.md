Santa Marta is a relaxed coastal city that serves as the perfect base for exploring Tayrona National Park and the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta. I enjoyed fresh seafood along the waterfront and day trips to secluded beaches and hiking trails. Tayrona's beaches are spectacular, but arrive early in the season to avoid crowds.

For culture, wander the historic center and try local Caribbean-influenced dishes. If you plan to trek to the Lost City (Ciudad Perdida), allow several days and book with a reputable guide.
