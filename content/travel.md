# Travel

Travel has been one of the most enriching experiences in my life. It's not just about seeing new places, but about immersing myself in different cultures, meeting people with different perspectives, and challenging my own worldview.

## Recent Adventures

### Japan (2023)
Exploring the contrast between Tokyo's futuristic cityscape and Kyoto's traditional temples. The food scene was incredible, and I loved learning about Japanese craftsmanship.

### Iceland (2022)
Driving the Ring Road and experiencing the otherworldly landscapes. The Northern Lights were a highlight, along with the geothermal pools.

### Italy (2021)
Touring Tuscany and learning about wine production. The history, architecture, and food were all amazing.

## Travel Philosophy

I believe in slow travel - taking the time to really get to know a place rather than just checking off tourist attractions. I prefer staying in local neighborhoods, eating where locals eat, and learning at least a few phrases in the local language.

## Photography

I document my travels through photography, both digital and film. You can see some of my travel photos in the [Film Photography](/film-photography) section.

## Travel Tips

1. Pack light - you'll thank yourself later
2. Learn basic phrases in the local language
3. Be flexible with your plans
4. Talk to locals - they often know the best spots
5. Keep a travel journal
